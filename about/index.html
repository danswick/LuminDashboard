DELTALUMIN

Helping you make smarter decisions about energy use.

Smart meters are digital meters being rolled out across Illinois, replacing your old electric meters. Everyone should have a smart meter by end of 2018.

Check to see if you already have a smart meter

DeltaLumin invites you to join a pilot program to test tools that may help you take advantage of the information from your new smart meter.

Take our survey to find out if you are eligible to participate. DeltaLumin will reach out and get you enrolled in our 4 month pilot, beginning in spring of 2015.

As a participant, we’ll work with you to access your smart meter information, and we’ll translate that information into your DeltaLumin account. Over the 4 month pilot period, you will be asked to take multiple 10-minute surveys and provide feedback on the program. There will be monthly opportunities for rewards as a thank you for your help.
